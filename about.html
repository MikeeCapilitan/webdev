About CWTSAIS

What is CWTSAIS?

The Civic Welfare Training Service Attendance and Information System (CWTSAIS) is a digital platform developed for Polytechnic University of the Philippines – Taguig Branch. It aims to modernize the process of managing attendance, announcements, and records for students enrolled in the Civic Welfare Training Service (CWTS) subject.

Purpose and Vision

CWTSAIS provides an organized and accessible way for both students and facilitators to monitor attendance and stay informed about events, community service activities, and announcements. Its purpose is to enhance efficiency, reduce paperwork, and promote active participation in civic welfare initiatives.

About CWTS

The Civic Welfare Training Service (CWTS) is one of the three components of the National Service Training Program (NSTP) in the Philippines. It focuses on activities that contribute to the general welfare and betterment of life for community members. Through CWTS, students learn the importance of social responsibility, leadership, and community engagement.
